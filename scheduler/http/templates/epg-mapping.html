Mapping and Ordering the EPG Channels:
The channels available in the EPG data have to be mapped to channels that TV Scheduler Pro knows about, this is so TV Scheduler Pro knows what real channel to use for each of the channels available in the EPG data. This channel mapping is then used when TV Scheduler Pro adds a schedule based on information from the EPG.

You can also set the order that the channels show up in the EPG and the order that the Auto-Add system searches through mapped channels by using the images to move the mapped channel up and down the list. Channels at the top are displayed first and searched first.

Only EPG channels that have been mapped to TV Scheduler Pro channels will show up in the TV Scheduler Pro EPG pages or be used in the Auto-Add system.
